About Me

As a pole dancer, I've always wanted an app to track my progress and moves.

Inspired by the PoleMovebook website, I built this app with offline support and privacy in mind. It doesn't collect any personal or usage data, and all your progress and move lists are stored locally on your phone. That said, this app provides an option to back up and restore your data to the cloud, if you need to.

I've extensively referenced the codes of points from POSA (Pole Sport & Arts World Federation) and IPSF (International Pole and Aerial Sports Federation) to appropriately determine the category and difficulty of moves. This app is designed to empower your pole dancing journey, whether you're a beginner or an experienced dancer.

If you're a software engineer, artist, or a pole instructor, and would like to collaborate, please reach out!
